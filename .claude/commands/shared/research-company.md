Use this before drafting outreach to a prospective sponsor/partner, as FORGE, HOST, GAVEL, or THRIVE.

## What to gather

- What the business does, and its rough size (employee count if findable).
- Any existing CSR/philanthropy activity or community involvement.
- A named contact or department if publicly findable (otherwise a general inquiries path).
- Social presence (Instagram/LinkedIn) — useful for personalizing the outreach.

## Match against your agent's targeting criteria (CLAUDE.md, Outreach Automation Workflow)

- **FORGE** — CSR programs, healthcare-adjacent, professional services (law/finance/consulting/real estate), tech, established Chicago brands, employees skewing 25–40.
- **HOST** — restaurants, wine bars, breweries, spirits, catering, hotel F&B; priority River North/West Loop/Lincoln Park/Wicker Park.
- **GAVEL** — experiences (spa/sports tickets/travel/golf), luxury goods, local artisans, businesses that can donate product or services.
- **THRIVE** — yoga/cycling/barre/Pilates/meditation/wellness brands, health-focused food/beverage; must fit the monthly W4AC event format.

## Output

A short structured write-up: company name, what they do, size, CSR angle (if any), contact, social links, and a one-line note on why they fit your tier/target. This feeds directly into drafting — don't research and draft in two disconnected passes.
